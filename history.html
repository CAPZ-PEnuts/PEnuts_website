How was CAPZ created ?

As it can be seen in the CAPZ's page, our group was created in the context of a school project.
But it wasn't that easy. Indeed, the project groups weren't created easily. Each student had to think with who he wanted to be for a complete year working on a project...
The creation of the group was done really fast. Indeed, we are all good in programming, we are all motivated and each one of us had something to add to a project like that.

  • Cloe (imagination & motivation)
  • Alexandre (Logical thinking)
  • Ziane (unity experience)
  • Pierrick (organization)

Why "PEnuts" ?

At the beginning we had such a lot of ideas - even to much - that we often said "faut pas partir en couilles..." (meaning in English : We can't go in all directions at the same time).
In this way, we found it funny to name our game with something like that.
Indeed PE are for "Partir En" and nuts stand for "couilles".Then both placed together gave penuts which is - as you may have noticed - not that far from peanuts.

Finding it so funny and describing so much our way of thinking that it became our game's name.
